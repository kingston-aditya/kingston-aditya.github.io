Research

My research interests lie in computer vision and machine learning. I want to build intelligent systems that perceive the world from images. Our world is complex, it is three dimensional and it is dynamic. Computational models get to observe this world from imagery but only partially as visual data does not capture the richness of the world we live in. The goal of my work is to design visual perception models that bridge the gap between 2D imagery and our 3D world.
